🎮 Squad Layout & Structure

Game Mode: Battle Royale Squad (4 active roster members). Squad limits allow a minimum of 4 up to a maximum of 6 players (including 2 substitutes managed strictly in between custom rounds). One designated captain per roster handles official desk communications.

📋 Eligibility & Ranks

All participants must prove Indian Nationality using valid legal identification documents (Aadhar Card, Passport, Student ID, or Driving License). Competing profiles require a minimum age of 16 years, profile Level 40+, and Platinum tier rank standard. Team names cannot be changed post-registration.

🚫 Anti-Cheat & Fair Play

The main communication grid is handled via DISCORD. Every registered team player must reside inside the official event channels. UV administration preserves full rights to modify rule layers to ensure fair sportsmanship execution.
