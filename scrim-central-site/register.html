Official Rulebook

🎮 Tournament Structure & Size

Game Mode: Battle Royale - Squad (4 players per team)[cite: 1]. Each team must maintain a minimum of 4 to a maximum of 6 players[cite: 1]. Up to 2 substitutes are allowed and need to be registered in the roster, but substitutions are only allowed in between matches[cite: 1]. One designated captain per team will be responsible for communication[cite: 1].

📋 Eligibility Restrictions

Players must be Indian Nationals to participate[cite: 1]. To prove lawful nationality, a player must present a valid ID document (Student ID Card, Passport, Aadhar Card, or Driving License)[cite: 1]. All players must be at least 16 years of age[cite: 1], level 40+, and Platinum rank minimum[cite: 1]. Team names cannot be changed after registering[cite: 1]. Primary communication is strictly handled via DISCORD[cite: 1].

🔧 Official Room Game Settings

  • Gun Attributes: OFF • Revival System: ON[cite: 1]
  • In-game Mission: ON • In-match Quest: OFF[cite: 1]
  • High-tier Loot Zone: OFF • Death Cam: ON[cite: 1]
  • Standardized costume for opponents active. Nicknames and elimination feeds hidden. Loadouts are allowed[cite: 1]. Maps used: Bermuda, Kalahari, Purgatory (decided randomly)[cite: 1].

⚠️ Disconnection & Delay Faults

Participants are not allowed to intentionally cause a disconnection during a game[cite: 1]. Remake or match restart is considered only under server failure or if 10 or more players disconnect simultaneously due to network issues[cite: 1]. If a team is not ready within 10 minutes of the scheduled time, they forfeit the match[cite: 1].

🚫 Serious Offenses & Penalties

Only mobile devices are permitted (no emulators)[cite: 1]. Screen recording must be done on mobile or external software; use of the in-game POV recorder is prohibited[cite: 1]. Zero tolerance for teaming, exploiting bugs/glitches, ringing, bribery, gambling, or vulgar/hateful language[cite: 1]. Violations draw warnings, point deductions, or direct tournament disqualification with prize money asset forfeit[cite: 1]. UV reserves the right to amend rules to ensure fair competition[cite: 1].
