Official Rulebook

🎮 Tournament Structure & Size

Game Mode: Battle Royale Squad. Roster layout requires minimum 4 to max 6 players (up to 2 substitutes registered, allowed only in between matches). One designated captain per team.

📋 Eligibility Restrictions

All competing squads must be Indian Nationals with valid Govt ID proof (Aadhar, Passport, etc.). Players must be 16+ years old, level 40+, and Platinum rank minimum. No team name shifts allowed post-registration.

🔧 Official Room Game Settings

  • Gun Attributes: OFF • Revival System: ON
  • In-game Mission: ON • In-match Quest: OFF
  • High-tier Loot Zone: OFF • Death Cam: ON
  • Standardized costume for opponents active. Nicknames and elimination feeds hidden. Loadouts are allowed.

⚠️ Disconnection & Match Delays

No intentional disconnections. Re-host or remake is considered only if server completely fails, or if 10+ players drop out simultaneously at startup. If a team is not ready within 10 minutes of scheduled time, they forfeit.

🚫 Strict Anti-Cheat & Compliance

Mobile/Tablet only (No Emulators). External screen recording required (No in-game recorder). Zero tolerance for teaming, hacks, macro/config edits, ringing, bribery, gambling, or unprofessional behavior.

💬 Main Communication Hub

The primary communication grid is DISCORD. Every player in the active squad must reside in the dedicated server channels post-registration.
